How to make Roleks

Description

Roleks is an East African street dish popularly invented by Ugandans. It is a delicious meal, just like a cheese sandwich or a hotdog, it is one very easy snack to make.

Ingredients

These are the Ingredients needed to make a Roleks

  • 2 eggs
  • Half a Tomato
  • Half an Onion
  • A bunch of Cilantro (dhania)
  • A 1/4 table spoon of salt
  • 1 cooked chapati
  • 2 table spoons of cooking oil

Preparation steps

These are the steps on how to prepare Roleks

  1. Beat the 2 eggs well and add salt
  2. Chop the cilantro (dhania), onion and tomato into small chunks
  3. Add the chopped cilantro (dhania), onion and tomato into the eggs
  4. Mix all of the well
  5. place your cooking pan on heated surface or stove or cooker for it to heat up for approximately 2 minutes
  6. Put the cooking oil ito the pan for 30 seconds
  7. Immediately pour in the egg mixture and let it cook until color changes to golden brown then remove your eggs from the source of heat
  8. Lay your cooked chapati on flat surface (preferably a clean flat plate) then place the fried eggs on it and roll

Enjoy your Roleks
